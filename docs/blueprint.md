# **App Name**: BevBooks

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Maintain a database of beverage products with name, SKU, category, and selling price (box/piece).
- Invoice Creation: Create invoices supporting billing by both boxes and individual pieces within the same transaction.
- Automatic Calculation: Automatically calculate total boxes, total pieces, discounts (percentage or flat), taxes (GST), and the final payable amount.
- Customer Database: Maintain a customer database with name, phone, address, and outstanding balance.
- Bluetooth Printing: Enable direct invoice printing to portable Bluetooth thermal printers and standard Bluetooth printers.
- Sales Reporting: Generate sales reports by day/week/month, including total sales.  Use an AI tool to produce insight regarding purchasing trends.
- Low Stock Alerts: Alert the user when product stock falls below a threshold.  Consider prompting user to generate restock order via email.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#29ABE2), evokes the feeling of refreshing beverages.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F8), offering a clean and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Orange (#F2994A), adds a touch of energy, to call attention to key functions.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font provides a modern look and a little warmth, for clear readability.
- Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clear, universally understood icons for navigation and common actions, such as printing and saving.
- Employ a simple, clean, and minimal layout with large, easy-to-tap buttons, optimizing for quick billing.
- Incorporate subtle animations for transitions and feedback, enhancing the user experience without being distracting.
